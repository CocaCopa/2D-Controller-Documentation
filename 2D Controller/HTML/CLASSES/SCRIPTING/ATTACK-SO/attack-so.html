AttackSO

Description

Description of the AttackSO scriptable object class goes here.


Public Properties
AttackName String literal of the attack name.
AttackIcon Sprite to be stored as the icon of the attack.


AttackAnimation The animation clip of the attack.
HitboxShape Define the shape of this attack's hitbox.
DamageableLayers Specify which layers can be damaged.
DamageAmount How much damage should be dealt by this attack.
ScalableDamage Determines if the attack damage should scale with the level of charge applied.
MinimumDamage Minimum damage of the attack.
Cooldown Attack cooldown in seconds.


DisableCastOnWall Whether or not your character should be able to cast the attack, if a wall is detected in front of them.
WallCastDistance Specify at what distance away from a wall your character must be, in order to allow them to cast the attack.
ResetVelocity True, sets your character's velocity to Vector3.zero when the attack is initiated. False, your character will continue moving at a constant speed based on their velocity before the attack.
CanChangeDirections Determines Whether or not your character can change directions while they're attacking.
AdjustPositionOnAttackEnd If your animation offsets your character during the attack, you can adjust their position by setting an offset. After the attack, your character will teleport to that offseted position.
CanMoveWhileAttacking Determines if the character should be able to move while they cast an attack.
AttackMoveSpeedPercentage Adjusts the character's movement speed as a percentage of their maximum speed.


AttackPushesCharacter If 'true,' a force will be applied to the character in the direction they are facing when the attack is initiated.
AttackPushMode Choose when your character should be pushed. OnInitiate: Once the attack is initiated. OnRelease: Once the attack is released.
ForceMode Choose which Rigidbody.AddForce() force mode should be applied.
Force How much force should be applied.
DelayForceTime Specifies the delay in seconds before applying force. The specified delay time should be less than the duration of the animation clip; otherwise, it will not be considered and the desired force will not be applied.
UseGravity Whether the character should be affected by gravity for the duration of the attack.
DragCoefficient Change the drag coefficient of the Rigidbody2D for the duration of the attack.


IsChargeableAttack Whether or not this attack can be charged.
InitiateChargeAnimation Animation to play when the attack is initiated. Can be null.
ChargeAnimation Animation to play when the attack is charging.
ChargeTime How much time should it take for the attack to be fully charged in seconds.
HoldChargeTime For how long should your character be able to hold their charged attack in seconds, before being forced to release/cancel it.
ChargeOverTime Specify the action to be taken when the attack is held for longer than the designated hold charge time.
CooldownIfCanceled Cooldown to set the attack on, if is held more than 'holdChargeTime' or if the attack is canceled.
CanMoveWhileCharging If 'true', your character will be allowed to move while they charge the attack.
ChargeMoveSpeedPercentage Adjusts the character's movement speed as a percentage of their maximum speed.
CanMoveOnReleaseAttack True if you want your character to be allowed to move as soon as the attack is released.


ThrowsProjectile Set to 'true' if your attack should throw a projectile.
ProjectileDamage How much damage should the projectile deal.
ScalableProjectileDamage Determines if the attack damage should scale with the level of charge applied.
MinimumProjectileDamage Minimum damage of the projectile.
ChooseRandomFromList Enabling this option allows multiple prefabs to be assigned as projectiles. Upon attacking, a random prefab will be chosen.
ProjectilePrefab Projectile to spawn.
ProjectilePrefabs Projectile to spawn.
ThrowAtPercentage Indicates the exact timing for launching the projectile during the 'AttackAnimation', based on a specified percentage.
DelayProjectileThrow Specifies the delay, in seconds, before the projectile is thrown.
